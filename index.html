Our Story

The Spirit of Oryx

Named after the resilient desert antelope, Oryx Leather was born from a passion for traditional craftsmanship and enduring design. Every piece we create tells a story of survival, elegance, and character.

We source only the finest full-grain vegetable-tanned hides. No machines, no shortcuts—just masterfully hand-cut, hand-beveled, and saddle-stitched heirlooms that develop a rich, personal patina over a lifetime of use.

Oryx Cardholder Slim Tan

NT$ 2,100

Crafted for those who appreciate simplicity, quality, and timeless design, the Oryx Cardholder Slim Tan is a minimalist everyday carry made entirely by hand from premium natural vegetable-tanned leather.

Its slim profile slips comfortably into a front or back pocket while still providing space for your daily essentials. Designed to carry 4–5 cards and 2–3 folded bills, it offers the perfect balance between functionality and minimalism.

Each cardholder is individually hand-cut, hand-stitched, and finished by hand, giving every piece its own unique character. The natural veg-tan leather will develop a rich patina over time, darkening and becoming even more beautiful with use, creating a wallet that is uniquely yours.
